# Auction Market Theory (AMT)

*Matt's trading automation is based on AMT principles*

## Overview

Auction Market Theory views markets as a two-way auction process where price discovery happens through the interaction of buyers and sellers seeking fair value.

## Key Concepts

- **Value Area** - Price range where ~70% of trading occurs
- **Point of Control (POC)** - Price level with highest volume
- **Initial Balance** - First hour's range, sets context for day
- **Market Profile** - Visual representation of time at price
